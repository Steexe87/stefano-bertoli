Software for Managing Measuring Instruments

This project involves the development of software dedicated to the management of measuring instruments within a company. The goal is to simplify the management process, improving accuracy while reducing time and intervention costs.

Key Features:

  • Easy and intuitive management of measuring instruments
  • Monitoring of calibration status for each instrument
  • Fast, simple, and cost-effective printing of calibration labels to apply to instruments

Technologies Used:

  • Microsoft Excel

Advantages:

  • The software, when used regularly within the company, improves the efficiency and management of measuring instruments, reducing management costs.
  • Microsoft Excel is software that every company already has at its disposal, so no additional third-party programs are needed to manage the measuring instruments.
  • Often in companies, calibration labels are an art-attack, costly in terms of time and money. The label printing method used by this software involves an inexpensive printer that works with readily available labels.
